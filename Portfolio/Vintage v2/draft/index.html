Our Story

VintageHauz Home Décor is a ceiling and interior renovation specialist assisting its clients to craft an affordable yet luxury interiors for their residential and commercial properties. It specializes in plaster ceiling, wood panel ceiling, moisture resistance ceiling, designer plaster ceiling, aluminum & strip ceiling, electrical & wiring works, ceiling down lights, ceiling and wall decorative interior and exterior lights, ceiling track lights, ceiling directional lights, interior and exterior seamless ambiance painting and etc. We do offer free interior design consultation and advice to our clients on coordination of mechanical and electrical services such as air conditioning, mechanical ventilation and plumbing. We guarantee our workmanship in crafting your dream project since our team possess extensive expertise for more than 5 years in this field. In addition to that, we only commit to use accredited construction materials & products by the relevant authority bodies such as Sirim, Bomba, CIDB, Singapore Green Label, American Society of Testing & Materials, European & British Standards. VintageHauz Home Décor closely liaise with its clients to capture the taste and customer preference, then indulge in crafting their affordable luxury dream interiors in a more valuable way. We strictly commit to the construction timeline as agreed and fulfill customer’s reasonable expectations. We cover projects in entire Selangor, WP Kuala Lumpur, Putrajaya and Negeri Sembilan for the ease of our customers.
